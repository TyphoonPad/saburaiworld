ACQUIRE LAND

Humanity has seen the rise and fall of many great empires. From the great Mongols to the powerful Sultans; from the prosperous Han dynasty from China to ever benevolent Mauryan Kings of India; from the mighty Imperial British to the great Democratic America manifesting its destiny. One resource that has ruled them all has been LAND. Behold my king for the land is scarce and would decide your fate here as well.

BUILD YOUR EMPIRE

What truly differentiates a king from a Lord is how well they grow their empire. While the ultimate goal is to go light-fold into a peaceful inter galactic META future, the journey is not going to be that easy. Scale your kingdom, build powerful armies, train your spies, educate your masses, start a religion, adopt social policies, use diplomacy to maintain good relations with your neighbors or maybe crush them and acquire their land. While leading an empire is fun in itself, we intend to reward our META rulers with $FFP tokens for every task done based on “proof of effort” and community voting.

UPGRADE your assets

$FFP tokens are used as a native currency for the platform for players. Users can stake their tokens to access legendary avatars, access META wargrounds, get discounts on in-game purchases, upgrade their kingdom and all this while still making passive income for themselves. Each Landowner becomes a validator on the platform.

trade with others

No empire in history has been able to flourish without trade routes. Satoshicity land owners would be able to trade, wager, negotiate, battle or use diplomacy with others within the bounds of their local empires or go global and trade with our partner metaverses. Lead your empire into a promising future with global alliances.

IMMERSE YOURSELF IN A FASCINATING, ADDICTING WORLD

  • SatoshiCity would initially run on a linear timeline starting 9000 BC having dragons, barbarians, Old Gods and Pharaohs to 3060 AD where we feel humanity would go intergalactic.
  • Own the land, start your empire, create parallel history, win world wars, become a superpower and create a legacy.
  • Form Strategies, own and trade land and create your ideal kingdoms.

UNLOCK LEGENDARY LEADERS

Legendary Leaders are the rarest resource in Satoshicity and each comes with their own unique supercharged attributes that are pivotal during the game. Genghis Khan helps increase your attack power and leads your army in battles while Einstein helps in increasing science output of your district and helps unlock new tech levels faster for your kingdom. Farm them all and use them as tradable liquid assets on the platform.

Why $FFP

Running a metaverse’s token economy is akin to running a small country. Without adequate monetary and fiscal policies, even if the metaverse is strong, the project still might be setting up for failure down the road. SatoshiCity’s monetary policy mainly pertains to $FFP token. It is the core utility token of the SatoshiCity platform. $FFP is structured as a fixed supply token and is a prerequisite for potential players and investors to buy for becoming a part of our world.
